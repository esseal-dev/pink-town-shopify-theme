Getting started with Posy

Posy is a soft, editorial Shopify theme by TriSoul Digital, made for boutique stores — jewelry, beauty, fashion, and gifts. This guide walks you through installing the theme, picking a style, and finding your way around the settings.

Install Posy

  1. From the Shopify Theme Store, open the Posy listing and click Try theme to preview it on your store, or Add theme to install it.
  2. In your Shopify admin, go to Online Store → Themes. Posy appears in your theme library.
  3. Click Customize to open the theme editor and start editing, then Publish when you are ready to go live.
Duplicate before major edits Before making big changes — and always before touching code — duplicate the theme (Online Store → Themes → ⋮ → Duplicate). If anything goes wrong, you can publish the copy and be back where you started.

How Posy's colors work

A style sets just five base colors, found in Theme settings → Colors:

  • Background — the page background.
  • Text — headings and body copy.
  • Borders — card outlines, dividers, and input borders.
  • Accent background — buttons, badges, and highlights.
  • Accent text — the text shown on top of the accent color.

Every other tone in the theme — soft tints, deeper accent shades, shadows, and placeholder colors — is derived from these five automatically. That means you can retint the entire store by adjusting a handful of pickers, and everything stays coordinated.

Fine-tuning After applying a style, you can adjust any of the five pickers individually in Theme settings → Colors. Your changes persist until you apply another style, which overwrites all five again.

Theme settings tour

All global settings live in Theme settings at the bottom of the theme editor's sidebar.

Favicon

Favicon image — the small icon shown in browser tabs and bookmarks. Use a square image, at least 32 × 32 px.

Typography

Two font pickers control all text in the theme: Heading font and Body font. Both draw from Shopify's font library, which includes system fonts (fastest) and a wide range of licensed web fonts.

Layout

  • Page width — choose Narrow or Wide for the maximum content width.
  • Page margin — the breathing room between your content and the edge of the screen.

Colors

The five base color pickers described above, plus Input corner radius, which rounds the corners of form fields, search boxes, and quantity selectors across the store.

Cart

Free shipping threshold — powers the free-shipping progress bar in the cart and cart drawer. See the Cart page for details, including the one rule that matters: it must match your store's real shipping settings.
